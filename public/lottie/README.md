# Welcome section Lottie animations

Replace the JSON files with your chosen Lottie animations. The home page welcome title shows the left animation on the left and the right one on the right of "Welcome to Eternal Sky Tour".

## Files

- **welcome-left.json** – e.g. "Couple camping in nature" from [LottieFiles](https://lottiefiles.com/free-animations/camping)
- **welcome-right.json** – **"Travel is fun"** by shivani bai:  
  [https://lottiefiles.com/free-animation/travel-is-fun-IYcVDUVlkE](https://lottiefiles.com/free-animation/travel-is-fun-IYcVDUVlkE)  
  On that page: **Download** → **Lottie JSON** → save as `welcome-right.json` here.

- **75808507-bf90-480c-9bd3-7c24ce1dfbf5.json** – LottieFiles animation used on the **right** side of the welcome title:  
  [https://app.lottiefiles.com/animation/75808507-bf90-480c-9bd3-7c24ce1dfbf5](https://app.lottiefiles.com/animation/75808507-bf90-480c-9bd3-7c24ce1dfbf5?channel=web&from=embed&panel=download&source=public-animation)  
  On that page: **Download** → **Lottie JSON** → save as `75808507-bf90-480c-9bd3-7c24ce1dfbf5.json` here.
